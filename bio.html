Biography

Born on January 29th, 1998, Grayson Garrett has always been driven by a deep curiosity for how things work. This innate interest naturally led to a passion for technology, from the intricate dance of software to the tangible world of hardware and microcontrollers.

Grayson's journey is one of continuous learning and hands-on application. Believing strongly in sustainability and self-sufficiency, he applies his skills to not only create but also to repair, repurpose, and empower others. Whether it's unlocking the full potential of a device, teaching others to become more tech-savvy, or finding value in discarded items, Grayson is dedicated to innovating practical and adaptive solutions for the dynamic challenges of our modern world.
