Biography

Born in January 1998, to Mother  Christine Belknap  aged 19 and Father Brian Garrett  aged 21, both of whom would go onto live very unstable lives, suffering from opiate addictions as well as eventually spending time incarcerated.

Grayson Garrett  was raised by his Maternal Great-Grandfather  Arthur Davis  a WWII Air/Army Veteran. Being raised by a Great-Grandparent   played a significant role in the views he holds on the world. He also spent weekends at his Paternal Grandparents  home where he would be introduced to the world of Video Games & Computers. Gary Marino  his Paternal Grandfather   was an early adopter of the custom gaming desktop. Utilizing xeon light bars before the popularization of LEDs in Gaming Computers of today. Seeing his paternal-grandfather  tinker with what to many can feel like a "black box" played a significant role in inspiring Grayson to aspire towards acareer in Computer Science. Challenging him to want to understand how things work, solving problems to better maintain and even alter systems around him to better suit his needs.This innate interest naturally led to a passion for technology, from the intricate dance of software to the tangible world of hardware and microcontrollers.

Grayson's life is one of overcoming challenges and learning through hands-on application. Believing strongly in sustainability and self-sufficiency, he applies his skills to not only create but also to repair, repurpose, and empower others. Whether it's unlocking the full potential of a device, teaching others to become more tech-savvy, or finding value in discarded items, Grayson is dedicated to innovating practical and adaptive solutions for the dynamic challenges of our modern world.
